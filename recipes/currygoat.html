Jamaican Inspired Curry Goat Shepherd's Pie

Description

A Jamaican favourite Curry goat and combined with an English favourite shepherds pie!

Required ingredients

  • 1kg Curry goat
  • 2 tbsp. Caribbean curry powder
  • Â½ tsp fresh ginger, chopped
  • 1 tsp. sea salt
  • 1 tbsp. Coarse black pepper
  • 1 tsp. ground pimento / all spice
  • 1 tsp. dried thyme
  • 2 tbsp. vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 250ml chicken stock
  • 125ml water
  • 6 fresh thyme sprigs
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper
  • 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
    • Potato topping
  • 6-8 floury potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 125ml Coconut milk
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp mixed herbs
  • 2 eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Grated cheddar cheese

Instructions

    1. Add curry goat to a mixing bowl then add curry powder, ginger, salt, pepper, dried thyme, pimento; mix well to coat then marinate in the fridge.
    2. Add oil to your pot on medium heat, then add onion and garlic; cook for 5 minutes until softened.
    3. Add curry goat then cook and brown for up to 10 minutes to get some color. Add chicken stock and a splash of water with fresh thyme; cook for 1.5 hours until the meat is tender.
    4. Add carrots and a scotch bonnet pepper 15 minutes before the end and cook until tender. Remove scotch bonnet and fresh thyme.
    5. Pre-heat oven to 160 degrees.
    6. Boil potatoes in salted water for 15 minutes until tender, drain well then mash potatoes with a potato masher. Add coconut milk, butter, eggs, and Parmesan cheese; mix together until smooth and creamy.
    7. Transfer curry goat into a 23 x 15 cm casserole dish then top with creamy mash and spread out evenly with a fork. Top with some cheese then bake for 30 minutes until golden brown.
    8. Enjoy!
